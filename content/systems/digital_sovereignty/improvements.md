# Improvements based om epidemiology

1) Cyber Epidemiological Surveillance: Create mandatory, near-real-time incident reporting; shared telemetry standards; cross-sector “sentinel networks”; and public risk dashboards (analogous to case surveillance + genomic monitoring).
2) Population Immunity Engineering: Treat patching and hardening as vaccination campaigns with coverage targets, boosters, and equity goals; reduce monoculture via controlled stack diversity so one exploit cannot infect most systems.
3) Outbreak Containment Protocols: Predefine digital NPIs (network quarantine, protocol throttling, feature shutdowns, privilege cordons) with legal triggers, decision authority, and rehearseable playbooks for rapid activation.
